About

Zoraida Cabrera-Mieles

I grew up in the little town of Hatillo, Puerto Rico. Where there are some nice beaches, but also, where the population of cows exceeds the population of people. In 2010, I left home to study in Boston University. The plan was to get a dual degree in English and Business. Instead, I graduated with a degree in English and History of Architecture. After college, I spent a few years working in customer service and giving tours in art museums. These experiences with art and people often find their way into my writing.

I still live in Boston. And I'm currently studying digital media design. Aside from writing short stories, I design websites, including this one.
